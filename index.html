The real math of a rental portfolio

It was never about how much you save.

Most people treat rentals like a savings account. Save for years. Buy one house. Save again. Buy another. It is the slowest path there is, and it quietly stops almost everyone before they build anything real.

The people who actually reach 50 doors are not saving harder or earning more. They buy at a discount and recycle the same dollars deal after deal. Your timeline is not set by your income. It is set by how good your deals are. Move the sliders and see it for yourself.

Educational estimates only. Not financial, tax, or investment advice.
